# KPI Framework

This project is built around the idea that operations dashboards should help leadership take action, not just observe trends.

## Core KPI Families

- Throughput and capacity
  - throughput attainment
  - availability
  - labor productivity
- Service execution
  - on-time service
  - perfect-order rate
  - expedite share
- Stability and risk
  - backlog pressure
  - incident rate per 1,000 orders
  - critical incident count

## Composite Score Logic

The site score balances:

- availability
- throughput attainment
- first-pass yield
- on-time service
- perfect-order rate
- normalized productivity
- incident pressure
- backlog pressure

The weighting is intentionally biased toward service reliability and flow stability, because those are the most visible outcomes for leadership teams.
